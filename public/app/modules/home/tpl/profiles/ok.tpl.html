Lieutenant Colonel (retired) Oral Khan JP
Chief Technical Director

Lieutenant Colonel Oral Khan is Chief Technical Director in the Ministry of Economic Growth and Job Creation, where he is accountable for the management of the policy portfolios of the ministry under the direction of the Permanent Secretary. Before making his career switch to the civil service in May of this year, Lt Col Khan completed over thirty one year of public service in a military capacity in the Jamaica Defence Force (JDF). During his military service, he also completed assignments on secondment to the Ministry of National Security and to the Cabinet Office. His past senior appointments include: Acting Inspector General, Headquarters JDF; Executive Director of the National Security Strategy Implementation Unit, Office of the Cabinet; Senior Director for Strategic Planning, Policy, Evaluation and Research, Ministry of National Security; Commanding Officer, Support and Services Battalion and Staff Officer, Operations and Training, Headquarters JDF. For his services to the military, he was awarded the Medal of Honour for Meritorious Service and the Medal of Honour for General Service in Grenada. Lt Col Khan holds a first degree in Management Studies and a Masters degree in Development Studies from the University of the West Indies, and a Masters degree in Military Arts and Science from the United States Army Command and General Staff College. He has published on the subjects of Community Protection in Inner-City Kingston and the Counter Terrorism Capacity of Caribbean Security Forces. He is married to Edith and together they have two adult sons, Daniel and Andrew.
