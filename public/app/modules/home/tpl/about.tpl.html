Overview

The Ministry of Economic Growth and Job Creation (MEGJC) was created in March 2016, with the change of the political administration. The Ministry is charged with drafting the blueprint to drive economic growth and sustainable development in Jamaica. The Ministry has responsibility for seven (7) critical portfolio areas. They are:

  • Land
  • Environment
  • Climate Change
  • Investment
  • Water and Wastewater
  • Housing and Works

Under its portfolio areas, the Ministry has oversight for some 48 Agencies, Departments and Divisions, which are responsible for approximately 68 subject areas. Included in the Ministry’s functions are the formulation and implementation of policy relating to these subject areas. It plays a monitoring role to ensure adherence to the policies, and that the operations of these entities are in accordance with the Government of Jamaica’s guidelines and regulations. The Political Directorate of the Ministry is headed by the Prime Minister, the Most Hon. Andrew Holness, O.N., M.P. He is supported by two Ministers Without Portfolio:

  • Hon. Dr. Horace Chang M.P.- Water, Housing and Works
  • Hon. Daryl Vaz M.P.- Land, The Environment, Climate Change and Investment
  • Hon. C. Everald Warmington has been appointed Minister of State in the Works portfolio

The Executive Management team at the Ministry is headed by Permanent Secretary, Mrs. Audrey Sewell J.P. The team also comprises the Director General, Dr. Sharon Crooks who is assigned to the Land, Environment, Climate Change and Investment portfolios. There are also two Chief Technical Directors:-

  • Lt. Col (Ret’d) Oral Khan provides support to the Director General in regard to the Land, Environment and Climate Change portfolios
  • Mrs. Doreen Prendergast, who provides direct support to the Permanent Secretary, in regard to the Water, Housing and Works portfolios

Support in the Investment portfolio is provided to the Director General by Mr. Omar Chedda, who is the Director of Investment. The Ministry operates from two primary locations:-

  • The Towers, 25 Dominica Drive, Kingston 5 and
  • 16a Half Way Tree Road, Kingston 5

However, it has divisional branches and agencies throughout the Corporate Area, and islandwide.

Vision Statement

A Jamaican economy which consistently meets or exceeds its growth targets, while providing meaningful jobs and economic opportunities for all in a sustainable manner.

Mission Statement

Provide visionary leadership, appropriate legislation, innovative policies and coordinated implementation which facilitate investments, sustained growth and prosperity for all in an environmentally sustainable manner.

Core Values

  • Professionalism - We commit to the highest standard of conduct in our interactions with internal and external customers.
  • Excellence - We commit to being the best at what we do at all times through innovation and self-development.
  • Efficiency - We commit to doing the right things right; to achieving the best results with available resources.
  • Environmental Stewardship - We commit to preserving the environment through policies, practices and public awareness to ensure healthy and comfortable living for this and future generations.
  • Teamwork - We commit to fostering a collaborative effort with all stakeholders to achieve the Ministry's goals.
  • Accountability - We commit to maintaining the highest levels of integrity and ethical standards through openness in all we do, with a view to maintaining public trust.
