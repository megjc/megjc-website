Mrs. Sharon Crooks
Director General

Since August 2012, Sharon Alicia Marie Crooks, with a sharp focus and quiet efficiency, has taken on the arduous task as the Director General at the Ministry of Economic Growth and Job Creation. Prior to her appointment as Director General, Dr. Crooks served as Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Local Government and Community Development (2011 to 2012) and Financial Secretary of the Ministry of Finance and the Public Service (2008 to 2009). Between those years, Dr. Crooks was selected to serve the Caribbean Region as the Caribbean Advisor to The World Bank Board of Governors in Washington DC, U.S.A (2009 to 2011). Dr. Crooks has also served as a Board Official for the Central Bank of Jamaica and as a member of the Jamaica Fiscal Responsibility Monitoring High-Level Team, responsible for the effective management of the public debt and the monitoring of the financial regulatory systems. The relatively youthful but vastly experienced and highly qualified public servant is a graduate from the University of Technology (Accounts and Economics) with a Doctorate in Organizational Leadership and a Master of Business Administration from the Nova Southeastern University, Florida in the United States. Dr. Crooks also holds Certification in Finance for Macroeconomists from the IMF, Washington DC, USA; Certification in Fraud Examination (CFE) from the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners, Texas, USA; and Revenue Agent Certification from the Tax Administration Jamaica.
